UWU DEFT Coders v4.0

We are hoping to give to chances to several teams for each university to participate in that competition. There will be about 200 participants. It will be 12 hours coding session, intended to provide students with an opportunity to demonstrate and improve their coding and problem solving skill.

All participants will be provided with refreshments throughout the event and other necessary facilities. We are hoping to grant participation certificates for all participants and cash awards for the winners.

Hackathon 2017


Participants & Teams

Each team should consist 4 undergraduates including at least one girl.

Coding session

Coding session has 12 hours with refreshments and funny activities

Location & Time

May 12 on 6.00 PM onwards at E1 hall.

Things to know..

Bring your laptops and power cords. Wifi will be provided, but bring backup connections.
